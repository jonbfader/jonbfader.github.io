Work History


FullStory

Jon was the first Support Engineer hired at FullStory and was tasked with growing and defining the practice. He designed buttery-smooth processes for triaging and escalating tickets to engineering, and for managing/updating the knowledge base. He developed a set of tools that helped support staff quickly diagnose issues with customer accounts and web integrations.

NCR

At NCR, Jon was a member of the Agile Technologies team, who were tasked with architecting, managing, and supporting the ALM toolstack for the company. Jon re-designed and automated large portions of the Point of Sale software release processes and managed the internal point of sale software database. He also lead the effort to transition point of sale systems to Windows 10 by working with stakeholders to architect and design the product images.

Haskell

At Haskell, Jon ran IT support and operations for the Atlanta branch of the company, which housed 60 employees and the company's disaster recovery data center. There, he provided direct and remote support to over 1,000 employees, wrote tools and scripts to make day-to-day support and systems engineering tasks easier, and helped integrate technology for the multiple companies that Haskell acquired while there.
